The Keyhole Laser Absorption Homepage

Datasets

The Keyhole Absorption Dataset

A fragment of the laser energy absorption datasets is openly available at the NIST Public Data Repository. Full absorption datasets can be made available upon reasonable request to Brian J. Simonds (brian.simonds@nist.gov).

The Keyhole Segmentation Dataset

Dataset for keyhole semantic segmentation is available through this google drive link.
Please note that our dataset is for non-commercial use only. Selling or licensing the dataset, or using it for commercial products or services is strictly prohibited.
